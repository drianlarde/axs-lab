When We Walk

SYNOPSIS.

Jason and Jase are introduced to each other in a world with barriers abound. The simplest things will separate them from each other. How can they make changes to have the tragic future not manifest itself? It is a story of loss, gains, and getting through unforseen challenges.

AWARDS AND ACCOLADES.

Received Best Documentary, CAAMFest; Received Vijay Mohan Social Change Award, Philadelphia Asian American Film Festival.
